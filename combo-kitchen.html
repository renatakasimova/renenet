Combo Kitchen

A companion app for looking up character rosters, move lists, and combos across six fighting games, designed and built end-to-end by renenet.

Beta testing on Google Play

Overview

Combo Kitchen covers Mortal Kombat 1, Street Fighter 6, Tekken 8, Guilty Gear Strive, Dragon Ball FighterZ, and Fatal Fury: City of the Wolves. Each game ships with a full character roster, move lists, and combo lists, so players can look up a character's kit or a specific combo without digging through a wiki or scrubbing through video guides.

It's built for players learning a new character, switching between multiple fighting games, drilling combo routes, or who just want a fast reference between matches or practice sessions. We developed the app from the ground up — product design, development, and QA. It's currently in beta testing on Google Play, with public release to follow.

Features

Rosters, moves, and combos

Full character rosters for six fighting games, with move and combo data for each character.

Combo lists

Organized by character, from basic bread-and-butter combos to more advanced routes.

Custom combo builder

Put together and save your own combo sequences for practice or reference.

Favorites

Save the characters and combos you use most for quick access.

Works offline

Combo and roster data is stored on your device, so you can check a combo list without a connection.

Built for quick lookups

A clean, dark-themed interface designed for use between matches or practice sessions.
