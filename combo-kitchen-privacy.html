Privacy Policy — Combo Kitchen

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Combo Kitchen ("the app") does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All content you create in the app — favorites and custom combos — is saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device: it is not transmitted to us, to any third party, or to any server, because the app does not connect to the internet or use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If you uninstall the app, this locally stored data is permanently deleted along with it.

Third-party services

None. The app contains no analytics SDKs, ad networks, or crash-reporting tools that transmit data off-device.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's functionality changes in a way that involves data collection in the future, this page will be updated accordingly, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
